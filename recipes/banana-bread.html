Banana Bread Recipe

Description

Easy to make banana bread. Makes 10 servings.

Ingredients

Here is a list of the ingredients you will need:

  • 3 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 cup of white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of melted butter
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon of baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Steps

Once you've prepared all the ingredients, follow these steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) and grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine the bananas, sugar, egg, and butter in a bowl. Mix the flour and baking soda together in a separate bowl, then stir in the banana mix until the batter is just mixed.
  3. Stir in salt and pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for about an hour until a toothpick inserted in the center of the bread comes out clean.
