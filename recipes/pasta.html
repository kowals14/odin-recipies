Pasta Recipe

Description

Quick and easy homemade pasta to use in your favorite dishes!

Ingredients

Here is a list of the ingredients you will need:

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 tablespoons of water (as needed)

Steps

Once you've prepared all the ingredients, follow these steps:

  1. Mix flour, eggs, olive oil and salt in a bowl until they combine. Add water 1 teaspoon at a time to the mixture until it forms into a smooth, thick dough.
  2. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured work surface and knead for 10 minutes, then let it rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Divide the dough into 8 balls and use a pasta machine to roll and cut the dough into shape.
