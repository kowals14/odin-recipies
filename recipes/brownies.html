Brownies Recipe

Description

Delicious brownies to enjoy! Serving size of 16 brownies.

Ingredients

Here is a list of the ingredients you will need:

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups of all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup of melted butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup of cocoa powder
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 cup of chopped walnuts

Steps

Once you've prepared all the ingredients, follow these steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (175 degrees C) and grease a 9x13 inch pan.
  2. Mix the sugar, flour, melted butter, egges, cocoa powder, vanilla extract, baking powder, and salt in a large bowl until they combine. Add in the chopped walnuts and then spread the batter into the prepared pan.
  3. Bake for about 20-30 minutes in the oven, until the top is dry and the edges started to pull away from the sides of the pan. Cool before slicing into squares.
